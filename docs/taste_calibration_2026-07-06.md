# Taste Calibration - 2026-07-06

This pass uses manual tagging made after the 2026-06-30 mass cleanup. Treat
2026-06-30 itself as a maintenance day, not as a clean taste signal. The useful
learning signal starts on 2026-07-01.

## Scope

- Recent MyTag links after the previous mass cleanup were reviewed.
- The useful examples are mostly from 2026-07-03 and 2026-07-06.
- The focus is on how the user marks Trance, Psy-Trance, Techno, Drum & Bass,
  Organic/Afro, Downtempo, and melodic vocal material.

## Lessons

### Trance / Psy-Trance

- Trance and Psy-Trance are not automatically Red.
- Vocal/emotional Trance can be Blue even when it is MAIN TIME.
- Psy-Trance can be Orange MAIN TIME when it is acid/dark/driving/hypnotic but
  still not the absolute peak.
- Classic-feeling or journey Psy-Trance can be Purple.

Examples:

- Laidback Luke, Jonathan Mendelsohn - Till Tonight: Red, rating 3, MAIN TIME,
  Driving/Female Vocal.
- Aly & Fila, Jwaydan - We Control The Sunlight: Blue, rating 4, MAIN TIME,
  Atmospheric/Driving/Emotional/Female Vocal/Hypnotic.
- Reborn, Zyce - Colors & Patterns: Orange, rating 3, MAIN TIME,
  Acid/Atmospheric/Dark/Driving/Hypnotic/Journey.
- Astral Projection - Let There Be Light: Purple, rating 4,
  Acid/Dark/Driving/Hypnotic/Journey.

### Peak-Time Techno

- Techno with Acid/Dark/Driving/Hypnotic often lands Red/MAIN TIME at rating
  3-4.
- Rating 5 is reserved; not every peak-time techno track should become 5.

Examples:

- Teenage Mutants, Kos:mo - Caught Up: Red, rating 3, MAIN TIME.
- Adam Beyer, Julian Jeweil - Loca: Red, rating 3, MAIN TIME.
- Oliver Huntemann, Kaufmann - K.o.: Red, rating 4, MAIN TIME.

### Melodic / Vocal Material

- PIAS melodic/vocal tracks can be Blue with PEAK or MAIN TIME tags when they
  are emotional/female-vocal-led.
- Siona/Miss Monique-style dark melodic material can be Orange MAIN TIME/PEAK
  instead of automatic Red.

Examples:

- Tinlicker, Hero Baldwin - God In You: Blue, rating 3, PEAK, Female Vocal,
  Emotional, Melodic Techno/Trance.
- Miss Monique, Glowal - Lost On A Dancefloor: Orange, rating 3, MAIN TIME/PEAK,
  Acid/Dark/Driving/Vocal.

### Downtempo / Opening

- Beatless or chillout mixes can be OPEN / INTRO even when BPM metadata is
  misleading.
- These can be Green or Aqua and should not be interpreted as high-energy just
  from BPM.

Examples:

- CamelPhat, Josh Gigante, Kuuda - So Good (Kuuda Chillout Mix): Green, rating
  1, OPEN / INTRO, Electronica/Organic Downtempo.
- Paul Ross, Lost Desert - Shooting Stars (Beatless Dream Mix): Aqua, rating 1,
  OPEN / INTRO, Organic / Downtempo, Atmospheric/Emotional/Female Vocal.

### Drum & Bass

- Vocal/female-vocal DnB often goes Pink MAIN TIME.
- Deep/atmospheric DnB goes Aqua JOURNEY.
- Dark/acid/hypnotic DnB can be Orange PEAK.

Examples:

- Freaks & Geeks, Nu-La - Space & Time: Pink, rating 4, MAIN TIME.
- Basstripper, Andromedik, A Little Sound - Sunlight: Pink, rating 3, MAIN TIME.
- Changing Faces - Emptiness: Aqua, rating 2, JOURNEY.
- High One - Waraome: Orange, rating 3, PEAK.

### Organic / Afro

- Organic/Afro with female vocal and hypnotic/journey tension can become Orange
  PEAK instead of Green.

Example:

- Eleonora, Ilias Katelanos, Plecta - Lose Control: Orange, rating 3, PEAK,
  Afro / Organic House, Female Vocal, Hypnotic, Journey.
